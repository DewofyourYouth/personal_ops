# Agenda Rules

Rules for how the bot proposes the daily agenda.

## Structure

- Propose 3–7 items per day
- Each item must be a single, independently completable action — never bundle two distinct activities into one item
- Return as a plain numbered list only — no preamble, no commentary

## Scheduling

- Schedule around calendar events — don't suggest deep work blocks that overlap with them
- Respect the alternating day structure (job search days vs. marketability days)

## Continuity

- If today's agenda already has open items, include them in the proposal
- Do not re-propose items already marked done or missed

## Calibration

- Use completion history to calibrate: if I consistently miss an item type, reduce its frequency or reframe it as smaller
- If I consistently complete something, keep it
- Adapt to my real capacity, not my ideal capacity

## Habits

- Do NOT include habits (defined in habits.md) as agenda items
- Habits are tracked separately and happen regardless of whether they appear on the agenda
- Only propose discrete, non-recurring tasks

## Friday Agenda 

- On Friday agendas should be very light.
- Mikva and Shnayim Mikra take a long time on Friday. I also need to help prepare. These can be tracked in habits not agenda.
- No serious work.
